Help & Support

Find answers to your questions and get the support you need for a seamless ReWear experience.

How to Sell

+

Steps to sell on ReWear:

  • Create an account or log in
  • Click "Sell" in the navigation menu
  • Take clear photos of your item
  • Fill out detailed listing form
  • Set a competitive price
  • Wait for potential buyers

Buying Guidelines

+

How to buy on ReWear:

  • Browse "Buy Now" section
  • Use filters to find your perfect item
  • Read item descriptions carefully
  • Check seller ratings
  • Make a secure payment

Swap Program

+

How our Swap Program works:

  • List items you want to swap
  • Browse other users' swap offerings
  • Propose a swap
  • Get mutual agreement
  • We facilitate secure shipping

Shipping & Returns

+

Shipping Details:

  • Standard shipping: 3-5 business days
  • Expedited shipping available

Return Policy:

  • 14-day return window
  • Items must be unworn and unwashed
  • Buyer pays return shipping

Account Management

+

Managing your ReWear account:

  • Update profile in Dashboard
  • Change password
  • Manage payment methods
  • Track sales and purchases
  • Edit listed items
